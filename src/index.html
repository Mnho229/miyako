About Us

The first MIYAKO opened in April 2001 in Lexington KY, and the second MIYAKO opened in May 2009 in Crescent Spring KY. We offer a Sushi Bar, Hibachi Grill Tables, and a Full Bar.

All the dishes are made-to-order, with Hibachi chefs cooking on sizzling Hibachi grills and master sushi chefs making making fresh sushi dishes before your eyes; only fresh ingredients will be used to create most of the dishes from scratch.

MIYAKO is committed to providing customers with an exceptional dining experience every time. We hope that you choose to have your next dining experience with us in the near future. Thank you.
